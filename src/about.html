About

Clippy is a minimal clipboard history log. I don't store your data, promise! Made with Electron, Preact, and htm. Open source @ kl13nt/clippy.

Tutorial

  • Copy an image from your web browser, or any text content from anywhere, and it'll show up in Clippy!
  • Left Click an entry to copy it back.
  • Right Click an entry to remove it from the log.
  • Click `Clear clipboard only` to clear your system's clipboard only, leaving the log as is.
  • Click `Clear log` to clear the log. You'll be prompted to clear your system's clipboard as well.
  • Click the `pin` icon next to each entry to pin/unpin it to/from the top of the log.
  • Hold `SHIFT` and Left Click multiple entries to select them then click `Copy Selection` to bulk copy your selections. Works with text only.
